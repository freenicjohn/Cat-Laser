## Why I Design This servo gimbal?

     The image is the most common servo gimbal in the shop, to be honest, it is very hard to use. It needs to cut servo horn to fit the hole and very unstable. 
     So i design a new one that you don't need to modify any part, the only thing you need to do is prepare 4 screws and 1 superglue.
    Hope you like it !
